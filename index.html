About Me

Hey there, I’m Pati. I’m a UX/UI designer with a passion for user-centric design. Over the past five years, I’ve worked as a UX/UI designer with top performance psychologists, gaining insights into what drives elite athletes and sports teams to achieve peak performance. This has given me a deep understanding of behaviour and mindset, helping me empathise with user motivations as a basis of every project I work on.

This experience shapes my design approach, showing me that the solution to a problem often lies in the simplest insights distilled from data. I use nudge psychology to promote long-term behaviour change, designing products that align with user goals and values. My aim is to subtly guide users towards positive decisions without limiting their freedom of choice.
